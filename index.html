FAQ

  • Wie zit er achter Noodz?

    +

    Noodz is a collaboration of two brothers: a dentist and a pharmacist. One question occupied them: why has it become so common that we use products with all sorts of unnecessary additives, packaged in an excess of plastic? They decided it was time to do something about it.

  • Wat is Noodz?

    +

    Noodz is a new line of honest oral care products. Soon we will launch the first Noodz: toothpaste that you can chew. One tablet of Noodz contains just the right amount to keep your teeth fresh and healthy. Want to try it? Mail to letstalk@noodz.nl!

    +

    Noodz is a counter movement. Noodz proves that it is possible to make smart and effective oral care products without unhealthy additives or polluting packaging. And to show that, we are open about everything we do. The naked truth!
